#LEVISOPENCALL

Levi’s Open Call is a global search for dancers and movers.

This is your chance to be in Kaytranada’s music video and work with the widely celebrated choreographer Sherrie Silver.

This is a paid opportunity, and travel expenses will be covered. You’ll learn the music video choreography, participate in photo shoots, and partner with Levi’s on more creative projects.

Hurry, submissions for #LevisOpenCall closes on April 11, 2024!

LEARN THE CHOREOGRAPHY

APPLY NOW

We're looking to celebrate individuals around the world who love to dance and move. All applicants must be 18 years or older with a valid passport and be able and willing to travel to the designated location for filming purposes. See the Submission Agreement for full eligibility requirement.

To apply, entrants must submit an audition video. To do so, follow these steps:

1

Show Us Your Moves

Record yourself following the choreography from Sherrie Silver’s video posted on her TikTok and Instagram and finish the dance with your own moves. We want to see you add your own flair.

2

Use The Track

Use this track “DRIP SWEAT” by Kaytranada ft. Channel Tres in your dance video. You can find the track on Levi’s most recently pinned post.

3

Share It

Share your video on TikTok or Instagram as a public post.

4

#LevisOpenCall

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How will the finalists be announced?
If selected as a finalist, you will receive an email, phone call or DM with more details.
When will I hear if I’ve been selected?
Selected dancers will be notified soon after April 11, 2024. Due to the amount of submissions, we are only able to respond to those who are shortlisted. If you don’t hear from us by then, it’s safe to assume your video was not a fit for this particular project.
Can I perform the dance with other people?
Yes! Your video can include more than one person. However, we want you front and center so we can focus on YOU. The other participants in the video must apply by posting their own video if they wish to be considered.
Do I need to wear Levi’s in my video?
We’d love to see you repping your Levi’s in your video, but it’s not a requirement for applying.
Is there any cost to apply?
Nope!
If selected, will I be responsible for any expenses during the shoot?
No, on-set and travel expenses including accommodation will be covered by Levi’s (and this is a paid opportunity.)
What if I’m not a professional dancer?
Great! We want to see people from all over the world with all types of dance styles and levels of experience. You don’t have to be a professional, you just need to move in your style.
If selected, what kind of content will I be asked to create?
We’d love for you to create a few pieces of content that show the world what your experience was like as a #LevisOpenCall dancer. Don’t worry, we’ll give you plenty of guidance and tools!
What are the dates of the music video shoot?
Finalists should be available for filming from April 17, 2024—April 26, 2024
Do I need to live in the US or speak fluent English to apply?
Nope! We encourage you to apply regardless of where you’re from or what language you speak. See submission agreement for more details.
Do I need a Visa?
This is dependent on where we travel for the production. If selected, we’ll be in touch to make sure you have all the necessary documentation. You will, however, need a valid passport.
